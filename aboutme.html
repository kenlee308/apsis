The Founder-Engineer Narrative

Bridging Applied Physics and Industrial Automation with AI.

I am **Kenny Lee**, an Atlanta-based founder-engineer with a background in **Applied Physics** and a decade of experience in the industrial motor and power systems sectors. My journey is defined by a transition from the physical logic of industrial automation to the digital reasoning of **Agentic AI**.

My early years in IT support and industrial electrical components (WorldWide Electric, Techtop) taught me the value of resilient, reliable systems. Today, I apply that same "no-nonsense" industrial mindset to AI development. I don't build toys; I build tools for the **Golden Age of AI Literacy**.

"Question Everything. Build the Future. Together."

Core Philosophy

Human-AI Symbiosis

I believe the future isn't about AI replacing humans, but about building the "linchpin" tools that allow workforce professionals—from shop floors to research labs—to navigate an AI-driven economy with confidence.

Local-First Ethics

Privacy and data governance are the cornerstones of trust. My projects (LocalGravity, Memochat) focus on local-first processing to ensure that the user remains the owner of their intelligence.

Workforce & Grant Alignment

M projects are developed in alignment with the **U.S. Department of Labor (DOL) AI Literacy Framework**. I am committed to:

  • Accelerating effective AI skill development across technical industries.
  • Providing open-source resources for workforce upskilling.
  • Pioneering safe and ethical implementation of autonomous agents in industrial contexts.
